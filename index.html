  • 2341.  What internet speed can I expect on installing JioExtend?

    What internet speed can I expect on installing JioExtend?

    JioExtend supports internet speeds of 25-30Mbps, where streaming HD Video content typically requires 10-15Mbps. This is keeping external environmental factors into consideration.


  • 6258.  What is the JioFiber Society Preview Offer?

    What is the JioFiber Society Preview Offer?

    JioFiber Society Preview Offer gives you Internet speeds upto 2 Mbps for 30 days, along with a monthly data quota of 5 GB. In case you consume 5 GB of your data quota within 30 days there will be no Internet services available.

    The plan will get auto renewed every 30 days.

    There are no installation charges taken as this is preview offer, but refundable security deposit of Rs. 4,500 needs to be paid for the ONT Home Gateway device.

    Amount can be paid using a credit/debit card or through a cheque in favour of ‘Reliance Retail Limited’.

    Please note: There are no Boosters/Top up/Recharge plans available under this offer

    FTR Tagging Details:

    Type Sub Type Sub Sub Type Others Information Given(FTR) FTTx Features and Price
